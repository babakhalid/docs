## Your Referral Link

### Getting Your Link

**Find Your Unique Link:**
1. Go to **Settings > Referrals**
2. Copy your referral link
3. Or generate custom links

**Link Format:**
```
https://yomake.ai/ref/YOUR-CODE
```

**Your Code:**
- Unique to you
- Never changes
- Trackable
- Shareable everywhere

### Customizing Your Link

**Create Custom Links:**
1. Go to **Referrals > Custom Links**
2. Click **"Create Custom Link"**
3. Add tracking parameter (e.g., `twitter`, `blog`, `email`)
4. Generate link

**Example:**
```
https://yomake.ai/ref/YOUR-CODE?via=twitter
https://yomake.ai/ref/YOUR-CODE?via=blog-post
https://yomake.ai/ref/YOUR-CODE?via=youtube
```

**Benefits:**
- Track which channels work best
- A/B test messaging
- Optimize referral strategy

## Referral Program Rules

### Eligible Referrals

**Counts as Valid:**
- New users who never had account
- Sign up using your link
- Subscribe to paid plan
- Pay for at least 1 month
- Real person (not bot)

**Does NOT Count:**
- Existing users
- Free plan only
- Signup without subscription
- Fraudulent accounts
- Your own alternate accounts

### Program Terms

**Fair Use:**
- No spam
- No fake accounts
- No misleading claims
- No paid advertising as Yomake
- Must disclose referral relationship

**Violations Result In:**
- Referral link disabled
- Rewards revoked
- Account suspension
- Legal action if fraud

## Troubleshooting

### Referral Not Credited

**Problem:** Someone signed up but no reward

**Solutions:**
1. Check if they subscribed (not just signed up)
2. Wait 24 hours (processing delay)
3. Verify they used your link
4. Check spam for notification
5. Contact support with details

### Link Not Working

**Problem:** Referral link gives error

**Solutions:**
1. Copy full link including `https://`
2. Test in incognito mode
3. Try different browser
4. Regenerate link
5. Contact support

### Rewards Incorrect

**Problem:** Token/credit amount wrong

**Solutions:**
1. Check referral's subscription level
2. Verify reward tier
3. Review activity log
4. Wait for processing (up to 24hrs)
5. Contact support

## FAQs

**How many people can I refer?**
- Unlimited! No cap on referrals or rewards

**When do I get rewarded?**
- Tokens: Instantly when they subscribe
- Credits: Within 24 hours

**Do referrals have to stay subscribed?**
- Initial reward: No, just first month payment
- Ongoing: Some rewards for continued subscription

**Can I refer someone who had free account?**
- Yes, as long as they never subscribed before

**What if they cancel after 1 month?**
- You keep all rewards earned
- They can resubscribe anytime

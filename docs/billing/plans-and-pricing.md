**Monthly reset:**

**Example:**

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Upgrade

1. Go to [Billing](/settings/billing)
2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
3. Select your desired plan
4. Choose monthly or annual billing
5. Enter payment details
6. Confirm upgrade

**What happens:**
- Upgrade takes effect immediately
- New features unlocked instantly
- Prorated credit for remaining time on old plan
- Token balance updated

### Proration

When you upgrade mid-cycle:

**Example:**
- You're on Free plan
- 15 days into monthly cycle
- Upgrade to Creator ($29/month)

**Billing:**
- Credit for unused Free days: $0 (free plan)
- Charge for Creator: $29
- Next bill: In 30 days for $29

**When you upgrade from paid to paid:**
- Get credit for unused time on old plan
- Credit applied to new plan charges
- Only pay the difference

## Downgrading Your Plan

### How to Downgrade

1. Go to [Billing](/settings/billing)
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select lower tier plan
4. Confirm downgrade
5. Changes take effect at end of current billing period

**What happens:**
- Keep current plan until billing date
- Downgrade occurs on renewal
- Lose access to premium features after downgrade
- Can still use existing projects (may need to delete some for Free plan)

### Before Downgrading

**Free plan limitations:**
- Only 2 projects allowed
- Must delete extras before downgrade completes
- Lose integration access
- Reduced token allocation

**Prepare for downgrade:**
1. Export code from projects you want to keep
2. Download any important data
3. Delete projects over the limit
4. Understand feature losses

## Cancellation

### How to Cancel

1. Go to [Billing](/settings/billing)
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select reason (helps us improve)
4. Confirm cancellation

**What happens:**
- Keep access until end of billing period
- No future charges
- Automatically downgraded to Free plan
- Projects and data preserved

### After Cancellation

**You keep:**
- All project code
- Deployed sites (remain live)
- Chat history
- Account data

**You lose:**
- Premium features
- Extra tokens
- Integration access
- Need to fit within Free plan limits (2 projects)

### Reactivating

Changed your mind?

1. Go to [Billing](/settings/billing)
2. Click "Reactivate Subscription"
3. Choose plan
4. Enter payment details
5. Resume immediately

## FAQs

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrade instantly or schedule downgrade for next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens to my projects if I downgrade to Free?**
A: You'll need to delete projects to fit the 2-project limit. Your code and deployments remain accessible.

**Q: Do tokens roll over?**
A: No, monthly plan tokens reset each billing cycle. Purchased tokens never expire.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We offer 14-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. Contact support.

**Q: How does proration work?**
A: When upgrading, you get credit for unused time on your old plan. When downgrading, changes occur at next billing cycle.

**Q: Can I pay annually?**
A: Yes! Annual billing saves 17% and includes 2 months free.

**Q: Do I need a credit card for Free plan?**
A: No, Free plan requires no payment method.

**Q: What happens if payment fails?**
A: We'll retry 3 times and send email notifications. After 7 days, you'll be downgraded to Free plan.
